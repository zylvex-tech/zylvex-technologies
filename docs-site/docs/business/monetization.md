## Creator Marketplace

The **Zylvex Creator Marketplace** lets users monetize their spatial content.

### How It Works

1. **Creators** publish anchor collections (e.g., "Best Hidden Spots in Berlin", "Art Walk: SOMA District SF")
2. Other users **purchase** access to the collection
3. Revenue is split between creator and Zylvex

### Revenue Share

| Creator Tier | Creator % | Zylvex % | Requirements |
|--------------|-----------|----------|--------------|
| Standard | **70%** | 30% | Any Pro/Team user |
| Verified Creator | **80%** | 20% | 1,000+ followers, 4.5+ avg rating |
| Featured Partner | **85%** | 15% | Invitation-only, exclusive content |

### Collection Types

| Type | Example | Typical Price |
|------|---------|--------------|
| Guided anchor tour | "Street Art: NYC Bushwick" | $4.99 |
| Local knowledge pack | "Authentic Tokyo: 50 Hidden Gems" | $9.99 |
| Mind map template bundle | "Product Launch BCI Session Pack" | $14.99 |
| AR experience | "Interactive Museum Guide: MOMA" | $2.99 |

**Payments:** Stripe Connect for creator payouts. Monthly payouts (min $20 balance). Supported in 40+ countries.

## AR Anchor Sponsorships

Physical-world advertising that is **contextual, non-intrusive, and valuable**.

### Sponsored Anchors

Businesses pay to place a branded anchor at a physical location. Unlike billboards:
- Clearly labeled as **Sponsored**
- Provide genuinely useful information (menu, offer, event)
- Are **opt-in** — users choose to view them
- Expire automatically — no visual pollution

### Pricing

| Format | Description | Price |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| Local anchor | Single GPS point, 30-day | $99/month |
| Venue anchor pack | Up to 10 GPS points, 30-day | $299/month |
| City anchor campaign | Up to 100 GPS points, 7-day | $1,499/campaign |
| Event anchor | Geofenced event area, 72-hour burst | $499/event |

### Target Advertisers

- Restaurants & cafés (menu anchors with daily specials)
- Retail stores (discount anchors near entrance)
- Event venues ("Event happening here tonight")
- Tourism boards and travel companies
- Real estate ("Open house this weekend")
